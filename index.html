Soil Sense Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Soil Sense collects, uses, and shares your information.

Information Collected

Soil Sense collects the following information: * **Soil moisture measurement data:** Soil Sense collects soil moisture measurement data from the ESP32 sensor via Bluetooth. This data includes the current soil moisture value, soil temperature, and measurement time. * **Device information:** Soil Sense collects device information, such as the model, operating system, and device ID. * **User information:** Soil Sense collects user information, such as name, email address, and country.

How We Use Your Information

Soil Sense uses your information for the following purposes: * **To provide the service:** Soil Sense uses your information to provide the soil moisture measurement service. * **To improve the service:** Soil Sense uses your information to improve the service, such as to identify trends and problems. * **To communicate with you:** Soil Sense uses your information to communicate with you, such as to send notifications about the status of the service.

Information Sharing

Soil Sense does not share your information with third parties except in the following circumstances: * **Service providers:** Soil Sense may share your information with service providers that help us provide the service, such as to host the app or process payments. * **Legal requirements:** Soil Sense may share your information to comply with legal requirements, such as to respond to a subpoena.

Your Rights

You have the following rights over your information: * **Access:** You have the right to access your information. * **Correction:** You have the right to correct your information if it is incorrect or incomplete. * **Deletion:** You have the right to delete your information. * **Portability:** You have the right to receive your information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. * **Objection:** You have the right to object to the processing of your information.
